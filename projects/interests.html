La La Land, and why it's my favourite movie

La La Land was made back in 2016 and remains one of my favourite movies. It is a musical that features an aspiring jazz musician and "soon to be" actress. The movie incorporates many forms of jazz and awesome melodies that will keep you hooked! The cinematography is absolutely perfect and just added more layers to my enjoyement towards the movie.

Main Cast

  • Ryan Gosling as Sebastian "Seb" Wilder
  • Emma Stone as Amelia "Mia" Dolan
  • John Legend as Keith
  • J. K. Simmons as Bill

I personally think that Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone as the main leads was the only option as those 2 were the only ones that could pull it off. Emma Stone also got an Oscar because of her performace in La La Land. As for Ryan Gosling, he played an amazing role and even took the time to learn the piano just for the movie. Overall, they both had amazing chemistry and gave life to the entire movie.
