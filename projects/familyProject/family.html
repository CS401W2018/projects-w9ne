Family of Supers are here to stay!

Mr Incredible (aka bob), is the leader and father of the incredibles family. He has incredible strength and incredible durability. He has the power to withstand many blows and is able to tank any form of damage. He is considered the tank of the family, as well as the brawns of the family.

Ms Incredible (aka Elastigirl), the mother of the Incredibles family. She is the wife of Mr Incredible and has the super power of being able to stretch incredible lengths. She can turn herself into anything, even a make shift boat. She is an incredible mother as well as a capable super hero. She is considered the brains of the family.

Dash (aka Speedo), is the son of both Mr and Mrs Incredible. He is the fastest in the family as he has super speed. He can run so fast that he can actually run on water. He can do this as long as he maintains his speed on water. He is practically the fastest and a very brave soul ready to jump into harms way. He is considered the motor and hot head in the family.
