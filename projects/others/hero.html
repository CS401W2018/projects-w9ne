My Hero's Name is Christopher Lloyd

My hero is Christopher Lloyd. He is someone I truly admire as an actor. He has played many roles of classic movies and has brought so much color and emotion into his characters. Not to mention, he has an amazing voice that is able to set the tone and demeanor of whoever he's playing. Although he did not play a role in La La Land, his influence in the movie industry is what shaped La La Land as an iconic movie today.

Chracter Highlights

Back To The Future - Dr. Emmett Brown
He was able to play the role of a "mad scientist" with a big heart. Back to the Future was a huge hit and gained a massive following with it's trilogy.
Anastasia - Grigori Rasputin
He played the antagonist in this movie and did some really well. He was able to showcase his amazing voice talent. This movie is a classic, and holds a special place in my heart.
Star Trek III: The Search for Spock - Kruge
Star Trek made an amazing decision in casting him in yet another antagonist role. This time he took on the mantle of a Klingon, where he made an iconic reaction as the tide of battle was thrown against him.
