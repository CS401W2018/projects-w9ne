USS Enterprise

The USS Enterprise is a starfleet vessel that has exploration in mind. It's mission is "To boldy go where no man has gone before". The voyages of the starship Enterprise comes with my new discoveries as well as many dangers lurking in the shadows. As the Enterprise goes on it's deep space exploration, it will meet many friends and foes, and form lightyear long relationships with new species.

Starfleet command has made many versions of this starship as upgrades to it's warpspeed as well as defense capabilities is paramount to deep space exploration. The image in this page shows one of the very first versions of the starship Enterprise (NCC-1701). The current Enterpise is at NCC-1701-J and it's fitted with the latest warp technology.
