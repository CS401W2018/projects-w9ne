What is positioning?

We are cave hunting today

1. What is the difference with Inline Text and Block Text?
- Block tags are usually used for bigger texts while Inline Texts are used for more smaller texts

  • Margins - Creates space outside something like an image
  • Border - Creates a line around our picture, between margins and padding
  • Padding - Creates space inside our picture

3. What are the units of measurement in CSS?
- We use px for pixels and even use other types of measurements like inches and such

4. When should we use a figure tag?
- We use a figure tag on media or photos that we want to highlight. We are also able to descriptions or captions for better explanations
